# [Bumrah T20 World Cup Magic Draws Massive Praise](https://8jjcricket.com/news/bumrah-t20-world-cup-magic-draws-massive-praise-rzw153)

India’s pace spearhead Jasprit Bumrah produced a brilliant spell in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup semi-final against England, playing a key role in India’s dramatic seven-run victory. The high-scoring encounter saw India post a massive total before holding off England’s powerful chase to secure a place in the tournament final. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Bumrah’s control during the crucial death overs proved decisive. In one of the turning points of the match, he delivered a tight over that conceded only a handful of runs when England were still within striking distance, forcing their batters to take bigger risks in the closing stages. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The Indian pacer’s performance drew widespread admiration across the cricketing world. England captain Harry Brook described Bumrah as “the best of all time at the minute,” highlighting how difficult it is for batters to score against his precise yorkers and calm execution under pressure. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Former South Africa captain Faf du Plessis also praised Bumrah’s influence, calling him a “superpower that any captain will dream of,” and comparing his impact to having a game-changing weapon whenever a breakthrough is needed. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

With India advancing to the final of the tournament, Bumrah’s match-winning spell further cemented his reputation as one of the most dangerous fast bowlers in modern T20 cricket.
