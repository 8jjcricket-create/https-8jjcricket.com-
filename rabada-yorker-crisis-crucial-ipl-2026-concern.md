# [Rabada Yorker Crisis: Crucial IPL 2026 Concern Explained](https://8jjcricket.com/news/rabada-yorker-crisis-crucial-ipl-2026-concern-mE0dpk)

In **IPL 2026**, fast bowler **Kagiso Rabada** has found execution of yorkers — a key weapon in his arsenal — noticeably erratic, raising concerns among analysts and fans alike.

### ⚠️ What’s Going Wrong  
Yorkers have long been a cornerstone of Rabada’s death‑over strategy. However this season, he’s struggled to consistently hit the block hole, often bowling fuller deliveries that are easier for batters to capitalise on.

### 📉 Impact on Team Strategy  
This dip in precision has led opposing batters to exploit the last few overs, turning potential tight finishes into freer scoring zones. Rabada’s team has felt the pressure as late‑innings run rates climb.

### 🧠 Technical vs. Mental Factors  
Experts suggest the issue isn’t purely technical. A combination of **rhythm disruption**, variance in run‑up timing, and increased match pressure may be affecting his release point at key moments.

### 🔄 Possible Adjustments  
Coaches are reportedly working with Rabada on fine‑tuning his wrist position and approach length to regain yorker accuracy. Match simulations have also been used to rebuild confidence under simulated pressure.

### 💬 Community and Analyst Reactions  
Fans and commentators have been vocal about the situation, with some calling on Rabada to use alternative death‑over tactics — such as slower balls and bouncers — until his yorker control returns.
