# [Shubman Gill Sets Sights on 2027 ODI World Cup as India’s Ultimate Goal](https://8jjcricket.com/news/shubman-gill-sets-sights-on-2027-odi-world-cup-as-indias-ultimate-goal-d5kPNx)

Indian batting star **Shubman Gill** has made it clear that India’s top priority in the coming years is to win the **2027 ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup**, expressing confidence in his team’s ability to achieve the ultimate goal in one‑day international cricket.

Gill highlighted that while India’s recent success in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup was a major achievement, the 50‑over World Cup remains the pinnacle for the team. With a strong batting lineup and a blend of experienced campaigners and young talents, India are widely regarded as one of the favourites for the 2027 tournament.

Speaking after India’s historic T20 World Cup triumph, Gill said that the team’s focus now shifts toward sustained excellence in all formats, especially the ODI World Cup on home soil. He emphasised that consistent preparation, tactical awareness and depth across the squad would be essential to lifting the trophy.

Gill also pointed to the importance of nurturing emerging players and building a cohesive unit capable of handling pressure situations on the biggest stages. His remarks underline confidence in the group’s ability to perform under pressure and replicate their shorter‑format success in the longer ODI championship.

As India prepare for a busy international schedule ahead, Gill’s leadership on the field and calm approach at the crease are seen as vital components of India’s progression toward their World Cup aspirations.
