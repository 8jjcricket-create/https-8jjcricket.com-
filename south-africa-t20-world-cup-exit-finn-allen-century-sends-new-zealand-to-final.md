The explosive innings from Allen shifted the momentum of the match early, putting the Proteas under immense pressure and leaving them struggling to contain New Zealand’s aggressive batting approach.

Allen’s remarkable knock not only propelled New Zealand into the final but also delivered one of the most memorable batting performances of the tournament. His ability to attack from the outset dismantled South Africa’s bowling plans and effectively sealed the result long before the closing overs.

For South Africa, the defeat represents another painful exit from an ICC knockout stage despite a strong run throughout the tournament. While the team showed resilience during the group phase, the semi-final proved to be a step too far against a New Zealand side firing on all cylinders.
