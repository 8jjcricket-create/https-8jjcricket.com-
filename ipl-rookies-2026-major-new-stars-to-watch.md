# [IPL Rookies 2026: Major New Stars to Watch](https://8jjcricket.com/news/ipl-rookies-2026-major-new-stars-to-watch-OYAXE0)

As the **Indian Premier League 2026** season gets under way, several young players and emerging talents are set to shine and potentially become tomorrow’s major stars.

Franchise scouts and coaches have identified a group of promising rookies who could make a significant impact, both with the bat and ball. These players have impressed in domestic T20 tournaments, age‑group cricket, and training camps ahead of IPL 2026.

### 🔥 Key Rookies to Watch

- **Vikram Singh** – A hard‑hitting middle‑order batter known for his power and timing, Singh impressed with multiple quickfire innings in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy.

- **Arjun Mehta** – A fast bowler with raw pace and bounce, Mehta’s potential to trouble top batters has drawn attention from several IPL franchises.

- **Devansh Patel** – A leg‑spinner who has shown great control of variations and flight, Patel looks ready to trouble batsmen on subcontinental pitches.

- **Rohan Iyer** – A dynamic all‑rounder with the ability to contribute both with the bat and ball, Iyer’s athleticism makes him a player to watch in pressure situations.

- **Sameer Shah** – A promising wicketkeeper‑batter whose consistent performances in age‑group cricket earned him an IPL contract for the first time.

### 💡 Why These Rookies Matter

These emerging players represent the future of franchise cricket, bringing fresh energy and excitement to the tournament. Their inclusion highlights the growing depth of talent in Indian domestic circuits — and the IPL’s role as a platform that can turn young prospects into global stars.

With a long season ahead, these rookies could play key roles in deciding matches and shaping their team’s fortunes. Fans will be keen to see who seizes their opportunity and makes a breakthrough in IPL 2026.
