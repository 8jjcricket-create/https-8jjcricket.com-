# Will Sutherland Calls Sheffield Shield Glory His Main Motivator Ahead of Victoria Final

Will Sutherland has underlined the emotional weight of Victoria’s Sheffield Shield campaign, declaring that leading his state to title success is the “main motivator” of his career as they prepare for the final against South Australia.

Sutherland, who has previously experienced heartbreaking final defeats, said those setbacks have only strengthened his desire to deliver a long-awaited Shield triumph for Victoria. The all-rounder has been central to Victoria’s dominance this season, both as captain and a key contributor with bat and ball.

The Victorian skipper has led a strong campaign built on consistency and depth, with the team finishing at the top of the table and earning a home final at Junction Oval. Their season has been marked by strong performances across the squad, including contributions from senior bowlers and emerging young batters.

For Sutherland, however, the motivation is deeply personal. Having played in multiple losing finals, he acknowledged the emotional toll but emphasised that those experiences have shaped his leadership and hunger for success.

Victoria now turn their focus to the decisive clash against South Australia, a side that enters the final with momentum and belief after a strong finish to the season. The match is expected to be tightly contested, with both teams carrying strong bowling attacks and in-form batting units.

As the final approaches, Sutherland’s message is clear: Victoria are not just playing for another trophy opportunity, but to complete a long-awaited redemption story under his leadership.
