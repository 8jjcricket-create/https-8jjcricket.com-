# [Riyan Parag Takes Over as Rajasthan Royals Captain for IPL 2026](https://8jjcricket.com/news/riyan-parag-takes-over-as-rajasthan-royals-captain-for-ipl-2026-SjmOSB)

**Rajasthan Royals have named Riyan Parag as their new captain for the 2026 Indian Premier League season**, signaling a major leadership transition within the franchise.

The appointment follows the departure of former skipper Sanju Samson, who was traded to Chennai Super Kings ahead of the upcoming IPL campaign. The move opened the door for Parag, one of the Royals’ longest-serving players, to step into the leadership role after several seasons as a key member of the squad. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Parag has been associated with Rajasthan Royals since his IPL debut and has steadily grown into an influential figure within the team. The all-rounder previously served as stand-in captain during parts of the 2025 season when Samson was unavailable, gaining valuable leadership experience that helped convince the management he was ready for the role full time. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The 23-year-old enjoyed a breakthrough campaign in the 2024 IPL season, scoring 573 runs at an impressive strike rate and establishing himself as a reliable middle-order batter. His performances marked a turning point in his career and reinforced his value to the franchise as both a player and a potential leader. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Rajasthan Royals’ decision reflects the franchise’s tradition of trusting young leaders. The team, founded in 2008, famously won the inaugural IPL title under Shane Warne and has often embraced bold leadership choices throughout its history. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Parag will now lead a squad that includes several experienced international players along with emerging Indian talent. With a blend of youth and experience in the lineup, the Royals will aim to challenge strongly for the IPL title in the upcoming season.
