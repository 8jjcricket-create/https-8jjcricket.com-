# [Patience And Leadership: Rohit Sharma's Journey To National Glory](https://8jjcricket.com/news/patience-and-leadership-rohit-sharmas-journey-to-national-glory-7jZ7sQ)

Former India captain Rohit Sharma was named among the 2026 Padma Shri awardees on Sunday, joining eight other past and present sportspersons on the prestigious Padma Awards honour roll. The 2026 list comprises five Padma Vibhushan, 13 Padma Bhushan, and 113 Padma Shri awards, with President Droupadi Murmu sanctioning a total of 131 awards, including two duo honours counted as single awards.
