# Matheesha Pathirana Injury Update: KKR Confirm Mid-April Return Timeline

Kolkata Knight Riders have received a significant update on Sri Lanka pacer Matheesha Pathirana, with the franchise confirming that the fast bowler is expected to return to action around mid-April during IPL 2026 after recovering from a calf injury sustained earlier in the year.

Pathirana has been undergoing rehabilitation under the supervision of Sri Lanka Cricket, and KKR management has opted for a cautious approach to ensure full fitness before integrating him into the squad. Head coach Abhishek Nayar indicated that while the bowler is progressing well, he is unlikely to be available for the opening phase of the tournament.

The franchise described his absence as a setback, given his importance as a death-overs specialist, but remains optimistic about his long-term impact once he returns to full fitness.
