# [Haris Rauf Named Marquee Player for Major Hundred Auction](https://8jjcricket.com/news/haris-rauf-named-marquee-player-for-major-hundred-auction-cu5diY)

Pakistan fast bowler Haris Rauf has been confirmed as one of the marquee players ahead of the upcoming **Men’s Hundred auction** in London. Rauf is among the top 10 names that will be highlighted early in the auction process, reflecting his high profile and value in the newly structured player recruitment system for the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The Hundred’s auction model — replacing the previous draft system — sees players ranked and tiered before being bid on by the eight franchises. Rauf’s marquee designation places him among the most sought-after international players, giving teams the opportunity to secure him early during the bidding. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

In addition to Rauf, several other Pakistani players have made the trimmed longlist of talent for the auction, with 14 from Pakistan still in contention after the initial registration period. Franchises emphasize that selections will be based on performance, availability, and team needs. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
