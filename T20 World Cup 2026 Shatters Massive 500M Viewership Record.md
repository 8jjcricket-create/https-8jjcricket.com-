# [T20 World Cup 2026 Shatters Massive 500M Viewership Record](https://www.8jjcricket.com/news/t20-world-cup-2026-shatters-massive-500m-viewership-record-eh988R)

The **ICC T20 World Cup 2026** has created history by becoming the **most-watched edition of the tournament**, crossing an incredible **500 million viewers** milestone. The achievement highlights the global popularity of T20 cricket and the tournament’s growing influence across digital and television platforms.

According to cricket authorities, the viewership numbers surged dramatically during the tournament’s key matches and group-stage clashes. The competition’s knockout phase has further intensified global interest as fans tune in to watch the world’s best teams battle for the title.

Digital platforms have also played a major role in the tournament’s success, with streaming services reporting record-breaking concurrent viewers during high-profile matches. The blend of traditional broadcasting and online streaming has allowed the tournament to reach audiences worldwide.

With the semi-finals and final approaching, the ICC hopes the tournament will continue to attract even larger audiences and set new records for cricket viewership.
