# [Harshit Rana Likely to Miss Most of IPL 2026 After Knee Surgery Blow](https://8jjcricket.com/news/harshit-rana-likely-to-miss-most-of-ipl-2026-after-knee-surgery-blow-oYRsjh)

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) have been dealt a major blow ahead of IPL 2026, with fast bowler Harshit Rana expected to miss a significant portion of the tournament following knee surgery.

Rana suffered the injury during a T20 World Cup warm-up match earlier this year and underwent surgery in February. He is currently undergoing rehabilitation under medical supervision, with no confirmed timeline for his return to competitive cricket. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The young pacer has been a key part of KKR’s bowling lineup in recent seasons, and his absence leaves a noticeable gap in their pace attack. His injury also ruled him out of the T20 World Cup, making IPL 2026 the second major tournament he is set to miss during recovery. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

With Rana sidelined, KKR will need to rely on other bowlers in their squad to step up and fill the void. The franchise is yet to confirm whether a replacement player will be named.

As the IPL season approaches, the team management will closely monitor Rana’s recovery, but his return is expected to take time, making his participation in most of the tournament unlikely.
