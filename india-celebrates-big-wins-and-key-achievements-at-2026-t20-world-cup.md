# [India Celebrates Big Wins and Key Achievements at 2026 T20 World Cup](https://8jjcricket.com/news/india-celebrates-big-wins-and-key-achievements-at-2026-t20-world-cup-wTeY3q)

The **Indian national cricket team** clinched the **ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026** title with a commanding **96‑run victory over New Zealand** in the final at the **Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad**, securing their **third T20 World Cup crown and becoming the first nation to defend the title successfully**. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The triumph triggered celebrations across the country, with fans, former players and public figures lauding the team’s dominant performance and historic achievement. Prime Minister **Narendra Modi** publicly praised the squad’s effort and highlighted the national pride sparked by the victory. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

India’s campaign featured standout individual contributions, including a powerful innings from **Sanju Samson** in the final and impactful bowling from Jasprit Bumrah, who was named Player of the Match for his 4/15 performance. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) added to the celebration by awarding the team a **substantial cash bonus of ₹131 crore (approximately $14.24 million)** for their historic achievement, in addition to the official ICC prize money. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Across India cities and towns, fans took to public spaces to celebrate the victory, with scenes of jubilant supporters marking an unforgettable moment in Indian cricket history. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
