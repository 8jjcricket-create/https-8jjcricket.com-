New Zealand’s batting lineup then stepped up with confidence, chasing the target comfortably. The top order played aggressive yet controlled cricket, ensuring the team stayed ahead of the required run rate throughout the innings.

The victory highlights New Zealand’s strong balance between experienced players and emerging talent, making them a serious contender for the T20 World Cup title.
