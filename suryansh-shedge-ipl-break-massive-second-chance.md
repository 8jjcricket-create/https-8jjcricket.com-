# [Suryansh Shedge IPL Break: Massive Second Chance for Young Talent](https://8jjcricket.com/news/suryansh-shedge-ipl-break-massive-second-chance-0Ecjjt)

Emerging Indian cricketer **Suryansh Shedge** has been handed a significant opportunity in the **IPL 2026** season, as selectors and franchise coaches move to give him his first substantial run in the tournament.

The 21‑year‑old batter, who impressed in domestic T20 competitions and youth cricket, was initially overlooked in earlier IPL drafts. However, after strong performances in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy and regional leagues, Shedge has been given a playing break that could lead to sustained appearances in the competition.

Franchise coaches have praised Shedge’s technique, temperament, and ability to handle pressure situations, noting that his inclusion offers fresh batting depth in the middle order. His selection is seen as a vote of confidence in young talent and a broader move by teams to look beyond established names when filling key roles.

Shedge’s opportunity comes at a pivotal time, as several teams seek to balance experience with emerging players capable of impacting games in critical moments. If he delivers on his potential, this second chance could accelerate his rise in Indian cricket and position him for future national consideration.

Fans and pundits have reacted positively to the news, expressing excitement about Shedge’s potential contribution and the emphasis on youth development in franchise cricket.
