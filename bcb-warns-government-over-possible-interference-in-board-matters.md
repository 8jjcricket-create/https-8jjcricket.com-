# [BCB Warns Government Over Possible Interference in Board Matters](https://8jjcricket.com/news/bcb-warns-government-over-possible-interference-in-board-matters-izpunR)

The Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB) has issued a strong warning to the government over possible interference in its internal affairs following the formation of an investigation committee to review the board’s recent elections.

The controversy stems from a decision by the National Sports Council to appoint a committee tasked with examining allegations of irregularities, manipulation, and abuse of power during the BCB’s 2025 elections. The board has expressed concern that such actions could undermine its independence and governance structure.

In an official statement, the BCB emphasised that it is a democratically elected body operating within its constitutional framework. It warned that any perceived external interference could raise serious concerns within the International Cricket Council (ICC), which maintains strict rules regarding government involvement in cricket administration.

The board has already initiated discussions with relevant authorities to clarify the intent and implications of the investigation. It also urged the government to reconsider the move and dissolve the committee in order to protect the stability and integrity of Bangladesh cricket.

BCB officials highlighted that similar instances of government interference in other countries have previously led to sanctions or suspensions by the ICC, making the situation particularly sensitive.

Despite the tension, the board has expressed willingness to engage in dialogue with the government to resolve the issue in a transparent and constructive manner, ensuring that Bangladesh’s standing in international cricket remains unaffected.
