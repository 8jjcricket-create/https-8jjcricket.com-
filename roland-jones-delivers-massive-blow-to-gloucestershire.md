# Roland‑Jones Delivers Massive Blow to Gloucestershire

In a **Rothesay County Championship Division Two match at Lord’s**, experienced seam bowler **Toby Roland‑Jones** delivered a sensational performance to put **Middlesex** firmly in command against **Gloucestershire**. Roland‑Jones was the standout bowler of the day, finishing with a superb **five‑wicket haul as Middlesex dismissed Gloucestershire for 216** in their first innings, creating a massive **229‑run deficit**. Gloucestershire were then forced to follow on and closed their second innings at **120/3**, still trailing by **109 runs** at stumps. 

The 38‑year‑old’s figures of **5‑37** highlighted his consistency and skill, as he dismissed key batsmen and helped cripple the Gloucestershire batting lineup. His bowling display was the decisive factor that allowed Middlesex to enforce the follow‑on and take control of the match early in the contest. 

Gloucestershire had shown some resistance through a spirited innings from **Miles Hammond**, who scored a fighting 77, but Roland‑Jones’ relentless pressure broke partnerships and kept the scoreboard ticking in Middlesex’s favour. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

This performance not only gave Middlesex a significant advantage in the match but also underscored Roland‑Jones’ enduring quality as a fast bowler in the County Championship. At a time when experience counts heavily in four‑day cricket, his ability to take crucial wickets proved pivotal.
