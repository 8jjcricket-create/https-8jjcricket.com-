# [Doggett Injury: Major Blow Before Sheffield Shield Final](https://8jjcricket.com/news/doggett-injury-major-blow-before-shield-final-sPts79)

Queensland fast bowler **Michael Doggett** has suffered a significant injury setback just ahead of the **Sheffield Shield** final, dealing a major blow to his team’s chances in Australia’s premier first‑class competition.

Doggett, who has been one of Queensland’s leading pace options throughout the season, sustained the injury during practice in the lead‑up to the Shield decider. Early assessments suggest he may miss the final, forcing selectors and team management to revise bowling combinations and plans.

The 26‑year‑old pacer has been in strong form this season, consistently troubling batters with aggressive pace and bounce. His absence will leave a notable gap at the top of the Queensland bowling attack, particularly in conditions where quick bowlers have historically made an impact.

Queensland coach praised Doggett’s dedication and work ethic, while confirming that the team will explore alternative options to cover his role. With several key matches remaining in the domestic calendar, the club will now rely on depth in the squad and the experience of other senior bowlers to shoulder responsibility.

Fans and commentators voiced concern on social media following the news, emphasising how crucial Doggett’s presence would have been in such a pivotal match. His fitness status will continue to be monitored, with hopes of a faster recovery now a key focus for both the player and the team.
