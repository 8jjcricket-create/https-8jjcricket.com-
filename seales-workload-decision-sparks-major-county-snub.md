# [Seales Workload Decision Sparks Major County Snub](https://8jjcricket.com/news/seales-workload-decision-sparks-major-county-snub-JduBC6)

Fast bowler **Kahler Seales** has been overlooked by a major English county side after prioritising workload management decisions tied to his franchise commitments, sparking debate about player availability and career balance.

Seales, who has been managing his workload carefully following a series of tight scheduling blocks, was reportedly snubbed by the county team that had previously shown interest in signing him for part of the English county season. Concerns around his availability — due to workload restrictions and rest protocols — meant the county opted to pursue other options instead.

The decision highlights the increasingly complex landscape for modern fast bowlers who must balance franchise T20 contracts, international obligations, and longer‑format opportunities. In Seales’ case, the focus on careful load management to prolong his playing career has come at the cost of a potential red‑ball stint overseas.

Experts say this might set a precedent where teams weigh the short‑term benefits of a player’s presence against long‑term fitness and scheduling constraints. For Seales, it means navigating expectations from domestic leagues, national selectors, and county sides while maintaining performance and fitness standards.
