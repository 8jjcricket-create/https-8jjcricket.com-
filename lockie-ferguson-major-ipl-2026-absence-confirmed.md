# [Lockie Ferguson Major IPL 2026 Absence Confirmed](https://8jjcricket.com/news/lockie-ferguson-major-ipl-2026-absence-confirmed-TYo5NN)

New Zealand fast bowler **Lockie Ferguson** will miss the early phase of **IPL 2026**, with his absence now officially confirmed as a significant setback for **Punjab Kings**.

Ferguson has opted to take time away from cricket following the birth of his child, prioritising family commitments before joining the squad later in the tournament. The pacer is expected to miss several opening matches, with reports suggesting he could be unavailable for the first few weeks of the season.

The 33-year-old has been a key part of Punjab Kings’ pace attack, known for his express speed and ability to deliver in crucial moments. His absence leaves a gap in the bowling lineup, forcing the team to rely on other fast bowlers to step up during the early stages of the campaign.

Punjab Kings management have supported Ferguson’s decision, highlighting the importance of player well-being and work-life balance in modern cricket. The franchise remains confident that he will return later in the tournament and make a strong impact.

Despite missing the start, Ferguson is expected to rejoin the squad for the latter part of IPL 2026, where his experience and pace could play a crucial role in the team’s push for playoff qualification.
