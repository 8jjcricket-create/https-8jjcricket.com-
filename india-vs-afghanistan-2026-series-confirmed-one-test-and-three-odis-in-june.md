# India vs Afghanistan 2026 Series Confirmed: One Test and Three ODIs in June

The **:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}** will host the **:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}** in a multi-format bilateral series in **June 2026**, the **:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}** has confirmed. The tour will feature a **one-off Test match** followed by a **three-match One-Day International (ODI) series**. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The Test match is scheduled to take place from **June 6 to June 10** at **New Chandigarh**, marking only the second time these two sides meet in the longest format of the game and the first Test at this venue. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Following the red-ball contest, the white-ball action begins with the first ODI on **June 14** in **Dharamsala**, followed by matches in **Lucknow** on **June 17** and **Chennai** on **June 20** — all set to start in the early afternoon. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

This series also marks the **first bilateral ODI series** between India and Afghanistan outside of multi-team events, presenting both sides with valuable preparation ahead of upcoming international commitments. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
