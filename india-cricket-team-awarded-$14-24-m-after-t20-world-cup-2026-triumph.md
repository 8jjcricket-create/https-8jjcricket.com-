# [India Cricket Team Awarded $14.24M After T20 World Cup 2026 Triumph](https://8jjcricket.com/news/india-cricket-team-awarded-1424m-after-t20-world-cup-2026-triumph-FCDSmW)

The **Indian national cricket team** was awarded a substantial **bonus of 1.31 billion rupees (approx. $14.24 million)** by the **Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI)** after claiming a historic victory in the **ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026**. This bonus is nearly **six times the official tournament prize money** and is among the richest rewards given to a World Cup‑winning side. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

India secured the title with a **96‑run win over New Zealand in the final** at the **Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad**, becoming the first team in history to **defend the T20 World Cup title** and win it on home soil. The triumph also marked India’s **third ICC Men’s T20 World Cup crown**, further cementing their status in the shortest international format. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The announced cash bonus of **Rs 131 crore** will be shared among players, coaching staff, and support personnel in recognition of their achievement, surpassing the reward given after India’s previous T20 World Cup win in 2024. In addition to the BCCI payout, the team also received the **official ICC tournament winnings** for their title triumph. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The record‑setting financial award reflects both India’s dominance in global T20 cricket and the Board’s desire to recognize the squad’s excellence after a commanding campaign. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
