# [Pathirana IPL Absence: Major Blow for KKR Ahead of 2026 Season](https://8jjcricket.com/news/pathirana-ipl-absence-major-blow-for-kkr-Cv1XEI)

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) have suffered a major setback ahead of IPL 2026, with Sri Lankan fast bowler **Matheesha Pathirana** set to miss the opening phase of the tournament.

Pathirana, known for his unique slingy bowling action and effectiveness in the death overs, is currently dealing with a fitness issue that has ruled him out of the early matches of the season. His absence leaves a crucial gap in KKR’s bowling attack, particularly in high-pressure finishing situations.

The young pacer has been a key performer in franchise cricket, consistently delivering breakthroughs and controlling the run rate during the final overs. His ability to execute yorkers and variations has made him a valuable asset in T20 competitions.

KKR management are now exploring alternative options within their squad to cover for Pathirana’s absence. The team is expected to rely on other pace bowlers and all-rounders to fill the void during the early part of the tournament.

Despite the setback, there is optimism that Pathirana will recover in time to rejoin the squad later in IPL 2026, potentially boosting KKR’s chances during the crucial second half of the campaign.
