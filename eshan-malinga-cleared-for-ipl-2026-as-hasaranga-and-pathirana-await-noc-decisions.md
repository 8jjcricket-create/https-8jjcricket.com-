# Eshan Malinga Cleared for IPL 2026 as Hasaranga and Pathirana Await NOC Decisions

Sri Lanka Cricket has officially granted Eshan Malinga the No Objection Certificate (NOC) for IPL 2026, clearing him to join Sunrisers Hyderabad ahead of the tournament. The decision confirms his full availability and allows the franchise to integrate him into their immediate preparations.

Malinga’s clearance follows fitness and workload assessments conducted by Sri Lanka Cricket, which continues to enforce a strict player management policy for those involved in overseas franchise leagues.

In contrast, Wanindu Hasaranga and Matheesha Pathirana are still awaiting final clearance. Both players remain under medical observation as part of ongoing injury management and workload evaluation programs.

Hasaranga’s situation is being carefully assessed due to recent fitness concerns, while Pathirana’s return timeline is being managed in coordination with Kolkata Knight Riders, who are expecting him to become available around mid-April.

Sri Lanka Cricket’s cautious approach reflects a broader strategy focused on long-term player availability, particularly with several key bowlers featuring heavily across global T20 leagues.

For Sunrisers Hyderabad, Malinga’s early approval offers stability in their bowling unit, allowing them to proceed with full-strength planning ahead of IPL 2026.
