# [Gautam Gambhir’s Tactical Masterclass Sends India to T20 World Cup Final](https://8jjcricket.com/news/gautam-gambhirs-tactical-masterclass-sends-india-to-final-rPZ07K)

**India secured a place in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup final** after edging past England in a thrilling semi-final encounter, with head coach Gautam Gambhir widely credited for a tactical masterclass that helped guide the team to victory.

India produced an explosive batting display to post a massive total of **253 runs**, powered by a superb innings from Sanju Samson. The aggressive approach set the tone for the match and placed immediate pressure on England’s bowling attack. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

England responded with a determined chase led by a sensational century from Jacob Bethell, but India held their nerve in the closing stages to secure a **seven-run victory** in one of the highest-scoring matches in T20 World Cup history. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Gambhir’s strategic decisions throughout the tournament have been widely praised. His flexible batting order, aggressive field placements, and calculated match-ups against England’s key players played a crucial role in tilting the contest in India’s favour.

The victory sends India into their **fourth T20 World Cup final**, where they will face New Zealand in Ahmedabad as they aim to defend their world title. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

For England, the defeat ends a strong campaign despite Bethell’s remarkable century and a spirited chase that kept the match alive until the final overs. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
