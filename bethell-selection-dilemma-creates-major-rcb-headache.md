# [Bethell Selection Dilemma Creates Major RCB Headache](https://8jjcricket.com/news/bethell-selection-dilemma-creates-major-rcb-headache-dR0u5b)

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) are facing a **selection dilemma** involving all‑rounder **Bethell**, which has created a headache for the franchise as they prepare for **IPL 2026**.

Bethell has been in strong form across recent T20 competitions, showcasing valuable contributions with both bat and ball. However, RCB’s squad balance and team combinations present challenges in determining how to best utilise him — particularly given the presence of several other multi‑role players competing for similar positions.

Team management are weighing questions about whether to deploy Bethell as a middle‑order batting option who can accelerate scoring, or to use him primarily as a bowling all‑rounder whose overs can provide strategic flexibility during the middle phase.

This dilemma has been compounded by pitch conditions, which vary significantly from venue to venue early in the tournament, forcing RCB to consider match‑by‑match selection strategies instead of a fixed role for Bethell.

Coaches have emphasised the importance of adaptability, with Bethell’s dynamic skill set seen as both an asset and a selection puzzle. How RCB resolves this question could influence their performance in close contests throughout the season.
