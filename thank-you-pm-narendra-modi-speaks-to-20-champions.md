# Thank You PM: Narendra Modi Speaks to T20 Champions

Prime Minister **Narendra Modi** congratulated **Team India** after their historic triumph in the **ICC Men's T20 World Cup**, praising the players for their determination, teamwork, and outstanding performances throughout the tournament.

India secured the title after a dominant campaign, defeating **New Zealand** in the final to claim their third T20 World Cup crown and successfully defend the trophy. The victory sparked celebrations across the country as fans hailed the team’s remarkable achievement. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Key Points

- PM Narendra Modi congratulated Team India after their T20 World Cup victory  
- India defeated New Zealand in the final to secure the title  
- The triumph marked India’s third T20 World Cup championship  
- Fans across the country celebrated the historic victory  
- Modi praised the team’s determination, skill, and unity
