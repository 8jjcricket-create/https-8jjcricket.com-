# Muzarabani Drops PSL for Massive KKR IPL 2026 Deal

Zimbabwe pace bowler Blessing Muzarabani has made headlines after withdrawing from the Pakistan Super League (PSL) to secure a contract with the Kolkata Knight Riders for IPL 2026.

The tall right-arm fast bowler was originally expected to feature in the PSL but decided to prioritize the IPL opportunity, joining KKR as a replacement signing for the upcoming season.

Muzarabani’s addition is expected to strengthen the franchise’s pace attack as the team prepares for another competitive IPL campaign. The move highlights the continued global appeal of the IPL, which often attracts top international players ahead of other leagues.
