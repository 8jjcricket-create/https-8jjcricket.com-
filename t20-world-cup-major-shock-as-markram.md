South Africa’s campaign at the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup ended in dramatic fashion after a heavy semi-final defeat to New Zealand. New Zealand chased down South Africa’s total of 169 with remarkable ease, winning by nine wickets. Following the match, South Africa captain Aiden Markram acknowledged that his team had a difficult night and credited New Zealand for their explosive start. Despite the disappointment, Markram remained proud of South Africa’s overall campaign in the tournament.
