# [Lockie Ferguson Major Absence as Star Misses IPL 2026 Start](https://8jjcricket.com/news/lockie-ferguson-major-absence-as-star-misses-ipl-start-xcxe5C)

New Zealand fast bowler **Lockie Ferguson** is set to miss the opening phase of **IPL 2026**, dealing a blow to his franchise ahead of the tournament.

Ferguson’s absence is linked to personal commitments, as the pacer has chosen to prioritise time with his family following the recent birth of his child. The decision has been supported by both his national board and franchise, who have emphasised the importance of player well-being and workload management.

The express fast bowler has been a key figure in T20 cricket, known for his pace and ability to deliver in high-pressure situations. His absence at the start of the season leaves a gap in the bowling attack, forcing the team to consider alternative options during the early matches.

Team management have indicated that Ferguson is expected to join the squad later in the tournament, once he is ready to return to competitive action. His availability for the latter stages could still play a crucial role in the team’s campaign.

Ferguson’s decision highlights the growing trend in modern cricket where players balance professional commitments with personal life, especially during demanding international and franchise schedules.
