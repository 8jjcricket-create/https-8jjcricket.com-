# Rohit Sharma Massive Leadership Moment Inspires MI Fans

Even sidelined by injury, Rohit Sharma reminded fans why he remains the heartbeat of Mumbai Indians. During MI’s dominant win over Gujarat Titans, a powerful leadership moment captured the imagination of fans and once again showed his influence beyond the playing XI.

With Suryakumar Yadav and Hardik Pandya on the field, Rohit stepped in as the on‑stand “captain,” sharing tactical insights and motivating the team from the dugout.
