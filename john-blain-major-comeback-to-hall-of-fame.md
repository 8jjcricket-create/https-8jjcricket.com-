# [John Blain Major Comeback to Hall of Fame](https://8jjcricket.com/news/john-blain-major-comeback-to-hall-of-fame-ZxBzca)

Scottish cricket legend John Blain has been reinstated into the Cricket Scotland Hall of Fame four years after his name was removed amid historical allegations of racism. Blain, who represented Scotland 118 times during his playing career, was inducted into the Hall of Fame in 2019 but had his honour suspended in 2022 during an independent review into past conduct in the game. Following a formal submission from the Hall of Fame Appointment Panel and approval from the Cricket Scotland Board, his suspension has now been lifted, subject to completion of equality and diversity training. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Blain described representing his country as “the greatest honour of my life” and expressed his gratitude to those who supported him throughout a challenging period. He said the reinstatement means a great deal not just to him but to those who stood by him during the process, reflecting the values of integrity, teamwork, and fair play that the sport instils. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
