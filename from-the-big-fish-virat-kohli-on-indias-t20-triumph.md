# From the Big Fish: Virat Kohli on India’s T20 Triumph

**Virat Kohli** praised **Team India** following their impressive victory in the **ICC Men’s T20 World Cup**, highlighting the team’s fearless cricket and strong unity throughout the tournament.

India secured the championship after a dominant campaign, defeating **New Zealand** in the final to claim their third T20 World Cup title.
