# Lahore Qalandars Massive Win in Rain‑Hit PSL Clash

In the **Pakistan Super League (PSL) 2026**, **Lahore Qalandars** produced a dominant performance to defeat the **Multan Sultans** by **20 runs** in a rain‑shortened, rain‑delayed encounter at the **Gaddafi Stadium in Lahore** on April 3. The match, reduced to **13 overs per side**, saw the Qalandars set a challenging target with a flurry of aggressive batting before holding their nerve in the chase.

Mohammad Naeem starred with a quickfire **60 off 28 balls**, anchoring Lahore’s innings alongside **Parvez Hossain Emon**, who contributed a lively **45 off 19 deliveries** to help the Qalandars post **185/5** in their allotted 13 overs.

Despite a competitive response from the Sultans — including a fighting **52* from Ashton Turner and 44 from Shan Masood — they were restricted to **165/5** in their chase, falling short of the target by 20 runs under the **DLS method**.

Lahore’s bowlers backed up the batting performance with disciplined efforts, applying pressure early in the Multan chase and ensuring that the Qalandars stayed ahead in the shortened contest.

This result marked a strong showing for the defending champions, strengthening their position in the PSL 2026 standings and underscoring their ability to perform in challenging conditions.
