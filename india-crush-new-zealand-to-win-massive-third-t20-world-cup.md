# [India Crush New Zealand to Win Massive Third T20 World Cup](https://8jjcricket.com/news/india-crush-new-zealand-to-win-massive-third-t20-world-cup-quQ1wM)

India produced a dominant performance to defeat New Zealand in the final of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026, securing a historic third title. Playing in front of a packed crowd at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, the hosts outclassed the Black Caps by 96 runs to retain the trophy and become the first team to win the tournament three times. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Batting first, India posted a massive total of 255 for 5 in their 20 overs. Sanju Samson led the charge with an explosive 89 off 46 balls, while Abhishek Sharma added a rapid half-century to push the total beyond New Zealand’s reach. The aggressive batting display gave India complete control of the match from the early stages. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

New Zealand struggled to keep up with the demanding target of 256 and were bowled out for 159 in 19 overs. Jasprit Bumrah delivered a match-winning spell with figures of 4 for 15, while Axar Patel supported him with three wickets to dismantle the Kiwi batting lineup. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The victory marked a historic milestone for India, making them the first team to win three men’s T20 World Cup titles and the first to successfully defend the trophy. Sanju Samson’s outstanding tournament run also earned him the Player of the Tournament award after scoring over 300 runs during the competition. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
