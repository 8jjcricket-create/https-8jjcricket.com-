# [Sanju Samson Believes India Will Win More World Cups After Historic T20 Triumph](https://8jjcricket.com/news/sanju-samson-believes-india-will-win-more-world-cups-after-historic-t20-triumph-L2kJoF)

India’s wicket‑keeper batter **Sanju Samson** believes that the Men’s T20 World Cup victory will act as a springboard for future success, and said he expects the team to compete for — and win — more ICC world titles in the years ahead.

Samson highlighted how the recent T20 World Cup triumph, where India defeated New Zealand emphatically in the final, would boost confidence across the squad and help build a winning culture. He pointed to the performances of both senior and emerging players throughout the tournament as evidence that India has the depth to remain a powerhouse in global cricket.

The right‑hand batter spoke about the importance of learning from key moments in major tournaments and using them to strengthen both skill and mental resilience. Samson also praised the young talents who stepped up during crucial matches, saying their contributions signal a bright future for Indian cricket.

Samson’s comments reflect growing optimism among fans and pundits that India’s cricket team can sustain high levels of performance across formats, including in the World Test Championship and the 50‑over World Cup scheduled for 2027.

He added that the Men’s T20 World Cup success has further increased interest in cricket at the grassroots level across India, with academies reporting rising participation among young players inspired by the team’s achievement.
