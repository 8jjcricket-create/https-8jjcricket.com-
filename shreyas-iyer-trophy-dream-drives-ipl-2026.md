# [Shreyas Iyer Trophy Dream Drives IPL 2026 Push](https://8jjcricket.com/news/shreyas-iyer-trophy-dream-drives-ipl-2026-push-eGvTrO)

India batter **Shreyas Iyer** has made it clear that his primary focus for IPL 2026 is to win a championship, using his ambition for silverware as a driving force ahead of the new season.

Iyer, who has been a consistent performer in the Indian Premier League over the years, believes that lifting the IPL trophy remains one of the biggest goals of his career. Despite strong individual performances, he is determined to translate that success into a team triumph.

The 30-year-old emphasised the importance of team unity and execution under pressure, noting that winning tournaments like the IPL requires consistency across all departments — batting, bowling and fielding.

Iyer also spoke about his personal development as a player and leader, highlighting the lessons learned from previous seasons where his teams came close but fell short of the title. He believes those experiences will be crucial in guiding his approach this year.

With IPL 2026 approaching, Iyer is focused on maintaining top form and contributing both as a senior batter and a leader within the squad. His hunger for success and clear target of winning the trophy adds extra motivation to his preparations.
