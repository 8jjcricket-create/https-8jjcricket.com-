# [Ishan Kishan to Captain Sunrisers Hyderabad in Pat Cummins’ Absence for IPL 2026](https://8jjcricket.com/news/ishan-kishan-to-captain-sunrisers-hyderabad-in-pat-cummins-absence-for-ipl-2026-fDCwdl)

India wicket‑keeper batter **Ishan Kishan** has been appointed captain of **Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)** for the 2026 Indian Premier League season after Australian pacer **Pat Cummins** was ruled out due to a long‑term injury.

Cummins, who was expected to lead SRH following his appointment as captain last year, suffered a back strain during pre‑season training and is set to miss the majority of IPL 2026. Sunrisers Hyderabad confirmed that Cummins will instead focus on rehabilitation with the franchise’s medical team. ([espncricinfo.com](https://www.espncricinfo.com/story/ipl-2026-ishan-kishan-named-srh-captain-pat-cummins-ruled-out-injury-1528481?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

In response, SRH turned to **Ishan Kishan**, who has been a pivotal member of their batting lineup in recent seasons, for leadership duties. The left‑hander brings experience and calm presence to the role, having captained India A in limited‑overs fixtures and led Mumbai Indians in the IPL previously at a young age. ([cricbuzz.com](https://www.cricbuzz.com/cricket-news/138012/ipl-2026-ishan-kishan-named-srh-captain-pat-cummins-ruled-out-injury?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

Sunrisers Hyderabad head coach expressed confidence in Kishan’s ability to lead, citing his tactical acumen and strong rapport with senior players such as Travis Head, Marco Jansen and Abhishek Sharma. The team’s management also highlighted Kishan’s calm demeanour under pressure as a key asset. ([cricbuzz.com](https://www.cricbuzz.com/cricket-news/138012/ipl-2026-ishan-kishan-named-srh-captain-pat-cummins-ruled-out-injury?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

The change in leadership is seen as a major moment for SRH as they aim to improve on their previous season’s performance and challenge for the IPL title. Kishan’s appointment adds another dimension to the team’s strategy as they balance youth, experience and tactical adaptability across the season.
