# ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026: Elite Match Officials Confirmed for Semi-Finals

The **:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}** is heading into its knockout stage, and the **International Cricket Council (ICC)** has confirmed the **Elite Panel of match officials** appointed for the **semi-final matches** of the 2026 tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The appointments feature experienced referees and umpires from the Emirates ICC Elite Panel, entrusted with ensuring fair play, expert adjudication, and smooth conduct across the high-stakes semi-final fixtures scheduled in **Kolkata and Mumbai**. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Among the appointments, **Richard Illingworth** and **Alex Wharf** have been named as on-field umpires for the first semi-final between **South Africa and New Zealand**, underscoring the ICC’s reliance on seasoned officials for pivotal matches. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The semi-finals are set to take place as the tournament reaches its climax, with cricket fans around the world tuning in for elite cricket action and major moments. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
