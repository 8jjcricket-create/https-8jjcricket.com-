# [Afghanistan Replace Rashid Khan with Ibrahim Zadran as T20I Captain](https://www.8jjcricket.com/news/afghanistan-replace-rashid-khan-with-ibrahim-zadran-as-t20i-captain-0gDXy3)

**Afghanistan Cricket Board has announced a leadership change**, naming **Ibrahim Zadran as the new captain of the T20I team**, replacing star all-rounder **Rashid Khan**.

The move marks a new chapter for Afghanistan’s white-ball strategy, with Zadran expected to bring stability and fresh tactical direction to the side.

Rashid Khan remains one of Afghanistan’s most influential players and is expected to continue playing a vital role in the team’s bowling attack.

The leadership change comes as Afghanistan prepares for upcoming international tournaments and seeks to strengthen its competitiveness on the global stage.
