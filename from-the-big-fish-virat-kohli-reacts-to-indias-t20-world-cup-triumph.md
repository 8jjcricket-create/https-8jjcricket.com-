# [‘From the Big Fish’: Virat Kohli Reacts to India’s T20 World Cup Triumph](https://8jjcricket.com/news/from-the-big-fish-virat-kohli-on-indias-t20-triumph-f8MlZN)

**Virat Kohli praised India’s remarkable T20 World Cup victory**, celebrating the team’s dominant campaign and congratulating the players for delivering a historic title triumph.

India sealed the **2026 ICC Men’s T20 World Cup** with a commanding 96-run victory over New Zealand in the final at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. The win secured India’s third T20 World Cup title and made them the first team to successfully defend the championship. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Following the victory, Kohli took to social media to applaud the team’s efforts. The former India captain described the performance as a phenomenal achievement and praised the aggressive brand of cricket played throughout the tournament.

“Champions. Phenomenal win for Team India in Ahmedabad. Absolutely no match for the explosive cricket played by us throughout the tournament,” Kohli wrote in his message to the team. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

India’s success was built on a combination of fearless batting and disciplined bowling. In the final, the hosts posted a massive total of 255 for 5 before their bowlers dismantled New Zealand’s chase, restricting them to 159. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Sanju Samson emerged as one of the standout performers of the tournament, playing decisive innings in the knockout stages and earning the Player of the Tournament award. Jasprit Bumrah delivered the decisive spell in the final and was named Player of the Match after dismantling the New Zealand batting lineup. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The triumph triggered celebrations across India, with former players, fans, and public figures praising the team’s dominance and the aggressive style that powered them to back-to-back T20 World Cup titles. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

For Kohli, the victory represented another milestone in Indian cricket’s recent golden period, reinforcing the team’s status as one of the most formidable forces in the global T20 game.
