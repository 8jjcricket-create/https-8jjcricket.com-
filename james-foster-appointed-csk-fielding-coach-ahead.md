# [James Foster Appointed CSK Fielding Coach Ahead of IPL 2026](https://8jjcricket.com/news/james-foster-appointed-csk-fielding-coach-ahead-of-ipl-2026-8hZl4Q)

Former England wicketkeeper **James Foster** has been appointed as the fielding coach of Chennai Super Kings (CSK) ahead of the IPL 2026 season, joining the franchise’s support staff under head coach Stephen Fleming.

Foster brings extensive experience from both international cricket and franchise leagues. He represented England across formats between 2001 and 2009 and has since built a strong reputation as a coach in T20 competitions around the world. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The 45-year-old has previously worked with Kolkata Knight Riders as a fielding coach and assistant coach, and has also been involved with teams such as Desert Vipers, Northern Superchargers and Peshawar Zalmi. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

At CSK, Foster will work alongside an experienced coaching group that includes batting coach Mike Hussey and bowling coach Eric Simons. His appointment comes as the five-time IPL champions look to rebuild after a disappointing 2025 campaign in which they finished at the bottom of the table. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The franchise has already begun preparations for the new season, with players attending pre-season training camps and management focusing on strengthening all departments, including fielding.

Foster’s arrival is expected to enhance CSK’s fielding standards and overall performance as they aim to make a strong comeback in IPL 2026.
