# [IPL 2026 Opener Set Without RCB vs PBKS Final Rematch](https://8jjcricket.com/news/ipl-2026-opener-set-without-rcb-vs-pbks-final-rematch-Bv8hGj)

The **IPL 2026 opener** is set to begin without a rematch of the dramatic **RCB vs PBKS final from the previous season**, with the league opting for a different blockbuster fixture to start the tournament.

Reports indicate that **defending champions Royal Challengers Bengaluru** are likely to headline the curtain-raiser on **March 28**, potentially against **Chennai Super Kings**, one of the league’s most successful and widely followed teams. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Meanwhile, **Punjab Kings**, who finished as runners-up in the 2025 final, are expected to begin their campaign a day later. They are likely to face **Gujarat Titans** in their opening match, giving both finalists separate spotlight moments during the opening weekend of the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The decision not to stage a final rematch immediately appears to be a strategic one, allowing the tournament to spread its biggest storylines across multiple opening fixtures while still ensuring high-profile clashes early in the season.

The **IPL schedule for 2026** is expected to be announced in stages, with the league coordinating match dates around upcoming state elections. Officials have also confirmed that captains and coaches will gather for a pre-season meeting on **March 25**, while the traditional captains’ photoshoot is scheduled for **March 15 in New Delhi** ahead of the tournament launch. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
