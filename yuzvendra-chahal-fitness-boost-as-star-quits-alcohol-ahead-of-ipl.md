# [Yuzvendra Chahal Fitness Boost as Star Quits Alcohol Ahead of IPL 2026](https://8jjcricket.com/news/yuzvendra-chahal-fitness-boost-as-star-quits-alcohol-ahead-of-ipl-tacOks)

India leg-spinner **Yuzvendra Chahal** has revealed a significant lifestyle change ahead of IPL 2026, confirming that he has quit alcohol in a bid to improve his fitness and extend his cricketing career.

The 35-year-old, who will represent Punjab Kings in the upcoming season, admitted that he has been sober for more than six months. The decision comes after a challenging period marked by injuries and inconsistent performances, which impacted both his physical condition and confidence.

Chahal explained that his focus is now firmly on maintaining peak fitness and giving his best on the field. He emphasised that as a senior player, he wants to set an example for younger teammates and contribute more effectively throughout the tournament.

The veteran spinner also opened up about previous injury setbacks, including fractures that affected his ability to bowl at full capacity during crucial matches. These experiences played a key role in his decision to prioritise his health and adopt a more disciplined lifestyle.

With IPL 2026 approaching, Chahal is entering the tournament with renewed motivation and a strong desire to perform consistently. His commitment to fitness is expected to play a crucial role in his comeback and in Punjab Kings’ campaign this season.
