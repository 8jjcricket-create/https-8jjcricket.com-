# T20 World Cup Debate: Massive Discussion Over BCCI’s ₹131 Crore Reward

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) recently announced a massive ₹131 crore reward for the Indian cricket team after their historic victory in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup. The announcement quickly triggered debate among fans, analysts, and former players. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

India secured the title with a dominant performance in the final, defeating New Zealand by a large margin to claim their third T20 World Cup trophy. The cash reward from the BCCI is significantly higher than the official tournament prize money, highlighting the board’s financial strength and its tradition of rewarding major achievements. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

While many praised the decision as recognition for the team’s remarkable success, others questioned the scale of the reward, leading to widespread discussion across the cricket community.
