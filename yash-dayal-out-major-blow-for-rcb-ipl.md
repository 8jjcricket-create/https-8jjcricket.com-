# [Yash Dayal Out: Major Blow for RCB in IPL 2026](https://8jjcricket.com/news/yash-dayal-out-major-blow-for-rcb-ipl-2026-ZoKovk)

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) have been dealt a significant setback ahead of **IPL 2026**, with pace bowler **Yash Dayal** ruled out of the season due to injury.

Dayal, a key component of RCB’s bowling attack, suffered a strain during pre‑season training and has been advised to miss the tournament to focus on rehabilitation and recovery. His absence leaves a notable gap in RCB’s pace options, particularly with other key bowlers already managing fitness concerns.

The 28‑year‑old’s injury comes at a crucial time, as RCB look to defend their title and maintain bowling depth across a long IPL campaign. Team management now face the challenge of readjusting combinations and relying on backup bowlers to fill the void left by Dayal’s absence.

Fans and analysts alike have expressed concern over the impact of Dayal’s unavailability, with discussions focusing on how RCB will manage their bowling rotations over the course of the season.
