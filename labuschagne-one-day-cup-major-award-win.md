# [Labuschagne One Day Cup Major Award Win](https://8jjcricket.com/news/labuschagne-one-day-cup-major-award-win-Itgd6l)

Australian international batter Marnus Labuschagne has secured a major honour after a remarkable campaign in Australia’s domestic One-Day Cup competition. The Queensland captain delivered a series of standout performances throughout the tournament, leading the run charts and playing a crucial role for his side across the season.

Labuschagne scored 436 runs during the competition at an outstanding average of 78 and a strike rate of 96.49. His season included four centuries and a highest score of 130, highlighting the consistency and dominance he displayed with the bat across multiple matches. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Votes from match officials across the tournament placed Labuschagne at the top of the standings for the prestigious award, finishing narrowly ahead of New South Wales batter Kurtis Patterson, while Tasmania all-rounder Beau Webster finished third. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Despite Labuschagne’s individual brilliance, Queensland narrowly missed out on a place in the tournament final, finishing third on the points table. Tasmania and New South Wales instead progressed to the decider, with the Tigers hosting the final at Bellerive Oval. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Labuschagne’s form in the competition further strengthened his reputation as one of Australia’s most reliable multi-format batters and underlined his importance for both Queensland and the national side.
