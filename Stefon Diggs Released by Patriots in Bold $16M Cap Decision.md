# [Stefon Diggs Released by Patriots in Bold $16M Cap Decision](https://8jjsports.com/stefon-diggs-cutby-patriots-in-bold-16mcap-decision/)

In a **surprising roster move**, the **New England Patriots released star wide receiver Stefon Diggs**, reportedly saving around **$16 million in salary cap space**.

The decision has sparked widespread debate among NFL analysts and fans, as Diggs has been one of the league’s most productive receivers in recent seasons.

Team officials emphasized that the move was part of a broader financial strategy aimed at building long-term roster flexibility.

While the release may open opportunities for younger players, it also raises questions about how the Patriots will reshape their offensive strategy in the upcoming season.
