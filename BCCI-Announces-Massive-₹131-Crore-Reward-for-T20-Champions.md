# BCCI Announces Massive ₹131 Crore Reward for T20 Champions

The **Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI)** has announced a **massive ₹131 crore reward** for the Indian cricket team after their historic triumph in the **ICC Men’s T20 World Cup**.

The announcement was made to recognize the outstanding performances of the players, coaching staff, and support team who contributed to the championship victory. The reward package is considered one of the **largest financial bonuses ever given to a national cricket team**.

According to reports, the prize money will be shared among the **players, coaching staff, selectors, and members of the support staff**, acknowledging the collective effort behind the tournament success.

India’s victory brought celebrations across the cricketing world, with fans praising the team’s determination, strong leadership, and match-winning performances throughout the tournament.

The BCCI’s reward announcement highlights the board’s commitment to honoring exceptional achievements in Indian cricket while motivating future generations of players to perform at the highest level.
