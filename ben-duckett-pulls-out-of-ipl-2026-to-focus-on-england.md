# [Ben Duckett Pulls Out of IPL 2026 to Focus on England Career](https://8jjcricket.com/news/ben-duckett-pulls-out-of-ipl-2026-to-focus-on-england-career-0HKyun)

England opening batter **Ben Duckett** has withdrawn from the **Indian Premier League 2026** season to prioritise his focus on international cricket and long‑term England commitments.

Duckett, who had been signed by an IPL franchise ahead of the new season, communicated his decision to the team management, emphasising his desire to concentrate on red‑ball and white‑ball duties for England. The batter cited workload management and preparation for key upcoming international series as key factors influencing his choice.

The 31‑year‑old has been an important part of England’s batting lineup across formats, and his decision reflects a growing trend among international players who are balancing franchise commitments with national team priorities.

Duckett’s absence will be felt by his IPL side, who had been counting on his experience at the top of the order. Team management are now exploring alternative options to reinforce their batting lineup ahead of the tournament’s start.
