# [Australia vs West Indies: King Shines Big in T20 Victory](https://8jjcricket.com/news/australia-vs-west-indies-king-shines-big-dmYYVV)

Australia secured an impressive victory over West Indies in their recent T20 clash, with **Alana King** emerging as the standout performer in a dominant all-round display.

Australia posted a competitive total after recovering from early setbacks, thanks to a strong partnership that stabilized the innings. The batting effort laid a solid foundation for the bowlers to take control later in the match.

King played a crucial role with the ball, delivering a match-winning spell that turned the game firmly in Australia’s favour. Her ability to strike at key moments and maintain pressure proved decisive as West Indies struggled to keep up with the required run rate.

The leg-spinner picked up multiple wickets in a tight spell, breaking important partnerships and ensuring Australia maintained control throughout the chase. Her performance highlighted her growing importance in the team’s T20 setup.

West Indies showed some resistance with contributions from their top order, but Australia’s disciplined bowling attack and improved fielding sealed a comfortable win.

The match served as an important preparation fixture ahead of the upcoming ICC Women’s T20 World Cup, with Australia continuing to fine-tune their squad and combinations.
