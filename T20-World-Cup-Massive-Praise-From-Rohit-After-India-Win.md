# [T20 World Cup: Massive Praise From Rohit After India Win](https://8jjcricket.com/news/t20-world-cup-massive-praise-from-rohit-after-india-win-cEkPFu)

**India captain Rohit Sharma** expressed immense satisfaction after India's impressive win in the **T20 World Cup**, praising the team for their discipline, confidence, and all-round performance.

Following the victory, Rohit highlighted the importance of teamwork and the contributions made by players across departments. From strong batting partnerships to disciplined bowling spells, the match showcased India's ability to perform under pressure in a global tournament.

Rohit also credited the team’s preparation and calm approach, noting that maintaining focus in high-pressure games is essential during competitions like the T20 World Cup.

The win further strengthens India’s momentum in the tournament, reinforcing their position as one of the strongest contenders for the title.
