# Prashant Veer Impresses MS Dhoni in CSK Nets Ahead of IPL 2026

Prashant Veer has emerged as one of the most talked-about young talents in the Chennai Super Kings setup after reportedly impressing MS Dhoni during recent net sessions ahead of IPL 2026. The youngster, who was signed at the auction for a significant fee, has been closely monitored by the CSK coaching group as preparations intensify for the upcoming season.

During the practice sessions, Veer showcased a composed batting approach, drawing visible approval from Dhoni, who is known for his sharp eye in assessing young players. His ability to rotate strike while also finding boundaries under pressure has added to growing optimism within the CSK camp.

Sources from the training setup suggest that Dhoni offered direct feedback to Veer on multiple occasions, highlighting both technical adjustments and tactical awareness. Such interactions are often seen as a strong indicator of a player being integrated into long-term squad planning.

CSK’s management has been focusing heavily on nurturing young Indian talent alongside experienced core players, and Veer’s early impressions have strengthened his case for more opportunities during IPL 2026.

While it is still early in the season build-up, the youngster’s rise in training sessions has already sparked interest among fans, with many viewing him as a potential breakout player for the franchise.
