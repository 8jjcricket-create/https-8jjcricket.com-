The veteran wicketkeeper-batter has already begun a **rehabilitation program**, with the franchise closely monitoring his recovery progress. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  

In his absence, CSK may rely on **backup wicketkeeping options and leadership support** from senior players to maintain balance in the squad.

Despite this setback, fans remain hopeful that the CSK icon will return soon and make an impact later in the tournament.
