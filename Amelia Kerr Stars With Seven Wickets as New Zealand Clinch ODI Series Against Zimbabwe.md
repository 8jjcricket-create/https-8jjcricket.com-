# [Amelia Kerr Stars With Seven Wickets as New Zealand Clinch ODI Series Against Zimbabwe](https://8jjcricket.com/news/amelia-kerr-stars-with-seven-wickets-as-new-zealand-clinch-odi-series-against-zimbabwe-jT0SiZ)

**New Zealand captain Amelia Kerr delivered a remarkable all-round performance**, claiming seven wickets to lead the White Ferns to a dominant victory over Zimbabwe and secure the ODI series.

Kerr’s extraordinary spell dismantled Zimbabwe’s batting lineup as she produced one of the best bowling performances in women’s ODI cricket. Her seven-wicket haul helped bowl Zimbabwe out cheaply and ensured New Zealand sealed the series with authority. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The performance also carried historic significance. Kerr’s figures set a new benchmark for a captain in ODI cricket, surpassing a long-standing record and reinforcing her reputation as one of the most influential all-rounders in the women’s game. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Earlier in the series, New Zealand’s batting had laid strong foundations, including a standout century from Brooke Halliday that helped the hosts dominate the contest. The combination of powerful batting and Kerr’s match-winning bowling proved too strong for Zimbabwe across the series. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Zimbabwe struggled to cope with New Zealand’s disciplined attack, with Kerr supported by fellow bowlers who maintained constant pressure throughout the innings. The victory confirmed New Zealand’s superiority in the three-match ODI series, which forms part of the ICC Women’s Championship cycle. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Kerr’s performance capped an impressive run of form for the New Zealand captain, whose ability with both bat and ball continues to play a central role in the team’s success on the international stage.
