# [Australia Deliver Dominant Win as Healy Bows Out in Perth Test](https://8jjcricket.com/news/australia-deliver-dominant-win-as-healy-bows-out-in-perth-test-UI4018)

**Australia produced a commanding performance in Perth**, defeating India by 10 wickets in the day-night Test at the WACA as Alyssa Healy brought her international career to an emotional close.

The hosts dominated the contest from the outset and wrapped up the match inside three days. India were bowled out for 198 in the first innings before Australia responded strongly with 323, establishing a decisive lead that shaped the remainder of the match. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Australia’s batting effort was highlighted by a superb century from Annabel Sutherland, who scored 129 to put the home side firmly in control. Ellyse Perry added a valuable 76 as Australia built a commanding advantage at the WACA. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

India struggled again in their second innings, managing only 149 despite resistance from Pratika Rawal and Sneh Rana. Australia’s bowlers maintained relentless pressure, ensuring the visitors were unable to mount a meaningful comeback. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Chasing just 25 runs to win, openers Georgia Voll and Phoebe Litchfield completed the task comfortably in fewer than five overs, sealing a dominant 10-wicket victory for the hosts. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The result carried extra significance as it marked the final international appearance of Alyssa Healy, who concluded a remarkable 16-year career with Australia. The wicketkeeper-batter, widely regarded as one of the leading figures in modern women’s cricket, was chaired off the field by teammates following the victory. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Although Healy managed only 13 runs in Australia’s first innings, the match served as a fitting farewell as Australia delivered a comprehensive team performance to ensure their captain signed off on a winning note. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
