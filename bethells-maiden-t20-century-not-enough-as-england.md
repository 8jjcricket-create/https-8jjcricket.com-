# [Bethell’s Maiden T20 Century Not Enough as England Exit World Cup](https://8jjcricket.com/news/bethells-maiden-t20-century-not-enough-as-england-exit-world-cup-DfdSY1)

England’s T20 World Cup journey came to a dramatic end despite a sensational performance from Jacob Bethell, who scored his maiden T20 international century in the semi-final against India. The thrilling match in Mumbai produced a massive run-fest, but England ultimately fell short as India secured victory by seven runs to reach the final. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

India posted a huge total of 253 for 7 after explosive batting led by Sanju Samson. The daunting target forced England into an aggressive chase from the start, setting the stage for a high-scoring contest that kept fans on the edge of their seats. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Bethell then produced a stunning counterattack, smashing 105 runs from just 48 balls with a flurry of boundaries and sixes. The young batter almost pulled off one of the greatest run chases in T20 World Cup history, but England finished on 246 for 7, falling just seven runs short of the target. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Despite the defeat, Bethell’s remarkable innings drew widespread praise across the cricket world. The 22-year-old showcased his growing reputation as one of England’s most exciting young talents, even though his heroics ultimately came in a losing cause. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
