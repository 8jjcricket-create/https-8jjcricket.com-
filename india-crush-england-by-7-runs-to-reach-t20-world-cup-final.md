# [India Crush England by 7 Runs to Reach T20 World Cup Final](https://8jj.com/t20-world-cup-india-crush-england-by-7runs-to-reach-final/)

**India secured a dramatic seven-run victory over England**, booking their place in the **ICC T20 World Cup final** after a tense semi-final showdown.

India’s batting unit set a competitive total, with key contributions from the middle order helping stabilize the innings during crucial phases. The team maintained steady momentum despite England’s disciplined bowling attack.

England’s chase started aggressively, but **India’s bowlers held their nerve in the final overs**, delivering tight spells that restricted scoring opportunities. The late wickets proved decisive in sealing the win.

The victory sends India into the **T20 World Cup final with strong momentum**, where they will aim to secure another major international title.
