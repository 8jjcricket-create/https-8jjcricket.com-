# BCCI Naman Awards 2026: Gill, Dravid and Mandhana Honored

The BCCI Naman Awards 2026 celebrated some of the most outstanding contributions to Indian cricket over the past year.

Among those honored were Shubman Gill, Rahul Dravid, and Smriti Mandhana, who were recognized for their performances and impact on the sport. The ceremony brought together leading players, coaches, and officials to celebrate the achievements of Indian cricket.

Events like the Naman Awards highlight the continued growth of the sport in India and acknowledge the dedication of athletes who have delivered memorable performances both domestically and internationally.

The awards ceremony also provided an opportunity to celebrate emerging talent and recognize the individuals helping shape the future of Indian cricket.
