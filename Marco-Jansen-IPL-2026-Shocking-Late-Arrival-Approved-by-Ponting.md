# [Marco Jansen IPL 2026 Shocking Late Arrival Approved by Ponting](https://8jjcricket.com/news/marco-jansen-ipl-2026-shocking-late-arrival-approved-by-ponting-KFhFD3)

Sunrisers Hyderabad’s pace spearhead Marco Jansen will join the squad late for IPL 2026 after receiving special approval from the team’s director of cricket, Ricky Ponting.

Jansen, who has been recovering from a minor shoulder issue sustained during the T20 World Cup, was initially expected to miss the start of the IPL season. However, Ponting and the SRH medical team have confirmed that Jansen’s fitness progress allows him to arrive late and still contribute effectively.

The decision comes as a boost for Sunrisers Hyderabad, who are navigating player availability challenges and look to utilise Jansen’s height, bounce and left‑arm pace in the middle overs.

Ponting stressed that the decision was made carefully, balancing Jansen’s workload with the demands of a long tournament: “We’ve monitored his recovery and believe he can make a meaningful impact even with a delayed start,” he said.

Jansen’s availability will add depth to SRH’s bowling unit, complementing other pacers such as Marco Jansen’s fellow quicks and providing tactical options across different pitch conditions in IPL 2026.
