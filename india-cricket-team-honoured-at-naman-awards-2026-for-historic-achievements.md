# [India Cricket Team Honoured at Naman Awards 2026 for Historic Achievements](https://8jjcricket.com/news/india-cricket-team-honoured-at-naman-awards-2026-for-historic-achievements-QWQwuf)

The **Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI)** held the **Naman Awards 2026** in New Delhi to honour India’s cricketing achievements across formats and age groups. A key highlight was the felicitation of the **Indian Men’s team** that won the **ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026**, along with several other title‑winning squads from recent ICC events — a historic first for Indian cricket. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The ceremony also recognised individual excellence and lifelong contributions to the sport. **Roger Binny** and **Rahul Dravid** were among the legends awarded *lifetime achievement honours*, while former India women’s captain **Mithali Raj** was celebrated for her enduring impact on women’s cricket. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

In addition to team awards, standout players like **Shubman Gill** and **Smriti Mandhana** received top honours for their performances in international cricket, reflecting India’s continued prominence in the global game. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The event underscored India’s dominance across formats and generations, celebrating both recent world titles and the contributions of cricketing greats in shaping the sport’s legacy in the country. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
