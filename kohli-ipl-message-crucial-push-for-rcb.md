# [Kohli IPL Message Provides Crucial Push for RCB Ahead of 2026 Season](https://8jjcricket.com/news/kohli-ipl-message-crucial-push-for-rcb-8rfpbE)

Star batter **Virat Kohli** has delivered an inspiring message ahead of **IPL 2026**, providing a crucial boost for Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) as they prepare for the new season.

Kohli, who played a key role in RCB’s historic title-winning campaign, emphasised the importance of maintaining hunger, discipline, and consistency as the team looks to build on its success. His message focused on staying grounded despite achievements and continuing to push for excellence.

The former captain highlighted that winning the IPL title was a major milestone, but stressed that sustained success requires even greater effort and commitment. He encouraged teammates to approach the new season with the same intensity and focus that brought them success previously.

RCB, one of the most popular franchises in the league, will be aiming to defend their crown after finally securing their maiden IPL title in 2025. The team enters IPL 2026 with high expectations and a strong squad built around experienced players and emerging talent. 

Kohli’s leadership influence, even without the captaincy, remains a key factor in the team’s mindset. His experience and consistency continue to inspire younger players, while his performances with the bat make him one of the most important figures in the squad.

As IPL 2026 approaches, Kohli’s message is expected to play a vital role in shaping RCB’s mentality, reinforcing their ambition to remain one of the dominant teams in the competition.
