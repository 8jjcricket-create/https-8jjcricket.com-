# Cameron Green Shocking Form Raises Concerns for KKR

The **IPL 2026 season** has not started as expected for **Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)**, with one of their biggest concerns being the underwhelming form of star all-rounder **Cameron Green**. Signed for a massive price at the auction, Green was expected to play a key role — but his early performances have raised serious questions.

## Poor Start with the Bat

Green has struggled to make an impact in the matches so far. Across his first few innings, he has failed to convert starts into meaningful contributions, often getting dismissed early and putting pressure on the middle order.

## Questions Over His Role

One of the biggest issues has been uncertainty around Green’s role in the team. He has been used in different batting positions, including No. 3, but has not looked comfortable. Experts have suggested that he may be better suited to a middle-order role rather than a top-order position.

## Lack of Bowling Contribution

As an all-rounder, Green’s value also comes from his bowling — but he has not contributed significantly with the ball. This has disrupted KKR’s overall team balance, as they are effectively playing without a full all-round option.

## Pressure of Price Tag

Being one of the most expensive players in IPL history has added extra pressure. Expectations were high, and his current performances have led to criticism from fans and analysts alike, increasing scrutiny on every outing.

## Impact on KKR’s Campaign

KKR’s struggles early in the season are closely linked to Green’s form. With the team already searching for its first win, they will need their key players — especially big-money signings — to step up quickly.

---

Cameron Green’s form remains a major talking point in IPL 2026. While there is still time to turn things around, both the player and the franchise will be hoping for a quick resurgence to keep their campaign on track.
