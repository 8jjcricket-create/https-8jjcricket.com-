# [‘Thank You PM’: Narendra Modi Speaks to T20 Champions After India’s World Cup Triumph](https://8jjcricket.com/news/thank-you-pm-narendra-modi-speaks-to-t20-champions-iI0eV6)

**India’s T20 World Cup triumph drew praise from Prime Minister Narendra Modi**, who personally congratulated the team following their dominant victory in the tournament final.

India defeated New Zealand by **96 runs in the 2026 ICC Men’s T20 World Cup final** at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. The win secured India’s third T20 World Cup title and made them the first team to successfully defend the championship. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Shortly after the victory, Prime Minister Modi shared a congratulatory message celebrating the team’s achievement. He praised the players for their exceptional skill, determination, and teamwork throughout the tournament.

In his message, Modi described the triumph as a proud moment for the country, stating that the victory had **“filled every Indian heart with pride and joy.”** He also highlighted the team’s resilience and commitment during the competition. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Members of the Indian squad responded warmly to the message, thanking the Prime Minister for his support and encouragement. The exchange reflected the nationwide celebration that followed the historic victory, with fans, former players, and public figures joining in to applaud the team.

India’s success was built on a powerful all-round performance in the final. The hosts posted an imposing **255 for 5**, before restricting New Zealand to **159**, sealing one of the most dominant wins in T20 World Cup final history. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The triumph marked another milestone for Indian cricket, reinforcing the team’s status as one of the leading forces in global T20 competition. With back-to-back World Cup victories and a new generation of match-winners emerging, the future of India’s white-ball cricket appears brighter than ever.
