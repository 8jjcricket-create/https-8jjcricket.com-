# [Bangladesh Sports Minister to Consult ICC Over BCB’s Future Amid Ongoing Probe](https://8jjcricket.com/news/bangladesh-sports-minister-to-consult-icc-over-bcbs-future-amid-ongoing-probe-cCc81x)

Bangladesh’s sports minister has confirmed that discussions will be held with the International Cricket Council (ICC) regarding the future of the Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB) as tensions rise over an ongoing government investigation.

The move comes after the government launched a probe into issues surrounding Bangladesh’s absence from the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup and alleged irregularities within the board’s administration. The situation has created uncertainty about the governance of cricket in the country.

Officials have indicated that any major decision regarding the BCB will be taken only after consulting the ICC, which enforces strict rules against political interference in cricket administration. The governing body’s stance makes the situation particularly sensitive, as violations could lead to sanctions or suspension.

The investigation is also expected to examine whether there were failures in sports diplomacy that contributed to Bangladesh’s withdrawal from the T20 World Cup. Government representatives have suggested that understanding these shortcomings is crucial before determining the next steps.

Meanwhile, the BCB has expressed concern over the probe, warning that external interference could damage Bangladesh’s standing in international cricket. The board has already engaged in discussions with ICC officials to ensure compliance with global governance standards.

As the situation develops, both the government and the BCB are expected to work toward a resolution that protects the integrity of Bangladesh cricket while addressing the concerns raised by the investigation.
