# [Manoj Tiwary and Irfan Pathan Question Abhishek Sharma’s Form Ahead of T20 World Cup 2026 Semi-Finals](https://8jjcricket.com/news/manoj-tiwary-and-irfan-pathan-question-abhishek-sharmas-form-ahead-of-the-t20-world-cup-2026-semi-finals-after-another-poor-outing-JIjhMi)

Former India cricketers **Manoj Tiwary** and **Irfan Pathan** have expressed concerns about **Abhishek Sharma’s recent form** ahead of the crucial **ICC T20 World Cup 2026 semi-finals**. After another disappointing outing, questions are being raised about the young batter’s consistency at a decisive stage of the tournament.

With the stakes higher than ever, experts believe that India needs stability and impactful performances from its top order. Tiwary and Pathan emphasized the importance of temperament, shot selection, and adapting to match situations in high-pressure knockout games.

As India prepares for the semi-final clash, all eyes will be on Abhishek Sharma to respond strongly and silence the critics with a match-defining performance.
