# [England Name 30-Player Intra-Squad Series Teams as Eight Uncapped Players Get Opportunity](https://8jjcricket.com/news/england-name-30-player-intra-squad-series-teams-as-eight-uncapped-players-get-opportunity-Ba2JFg)

England have announced a 30-player group for an upcoming intra-squad series in South Africa, giving several emerging players a major opportunity to impress the selectors ahead of the Women’s T20 World Cup. The squad will be split into two teams for a competitive series designed to simulate match pressure and evaluate player readiness. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Eight uncapped players have been included in the group, including Tilly Corteen-Coleman, Jodi Grewcock, Grace Potts, Grace Scrivens, Alexa Stonehouse, Davina Perrin, Rhianna Southby and Ellie Threlkeld. The series offers these rising talents the chance to showcase their abilities alongside established international stars. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The two teams will be named after England cricket legends Jan Brittin and Rachael Heyhoe Flint. England captain Nat Sciver-Brunt will lead Team Brittin, while vice-captain Charlie Dean will captain Team Heyhoe Flint as the squads compete in a series of matches in Pretoria. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Head coach Charlotte Edwards emphasized that the series will provide valuable preparation for upcoming global tournaments. She described it as a crucial opportunity for players to prove their value in competitive match situations and strengthen England’s squad depth ahead of a busy international schedule. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Originally scheduled to take place in Abu Dhabi, the training tour was relocated to South Africa due to the security situation in the Middle East. The matches will run from March 10 to March 27 as England fine-tune their preparations for the upcoming season. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
